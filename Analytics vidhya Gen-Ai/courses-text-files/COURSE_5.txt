Course title : Learning Autonomous Driving Behaviors with LLMs & RL

Title description : Learn to train autonomous driving agents using Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Large Language Models (LLMs). Gain practical experience designing AI systems that simulate safe, human-like driving behavior.
